  • How long will my order take?

    • All orders usually gets shipped within 5-7 working days. So you can expect a delivery between 7-10 days. For orders containing either phone cases / pop grips / t-shirts /wall arts / decals or custom products do expect a delivery between 12-15 days as these are made to order.

  • What should I do if I have received only a part of my order?

    • We make sure that all the products are sent to you in one package. Please mail at hello@gmail.com with the details of missing product but within 15 days of receiving your order. We shall check and get it sorted asap.

  • Do you ship internationally?

    • Yes we do ! :) Rs.4000 will be charged as shipping charge.

  • What do I do if I don't like the product I received ?

    • Please note that the products purchased can be returned (unopened / unused without damage and with all the tags intact) within 15 days of receiving the order and please pack the items securely to prevent any loss or damage during transit. You will receive a refund.

  • My tracking link shows 'delivered' but I have not received the package. What do I do ?

    • Please drop us a mail at hello@gmail.com within 24 hrs of the delivery mentioned in the tracking link. We shall check with the courier service.

  • Can I cancel my order once the product has been shipped?

    • Unfortunately, we cannot cancel orders once the shipment is out of our store for delivery.

  • Do you do customised products?

    • Hell yes! If your order quantity is sizable enough, then do contact us and we can make products.

  • When do I get my refund for the cancelled order?

    • If the payment was by credit/ debit card or net-banking, the money will be refunded to your credit/ debit card or net-banking account, respectively. Typically, refunds are processed between 7- 12 working days.
